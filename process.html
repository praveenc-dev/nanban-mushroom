Farming Process

From Spawn To Harvest

1

Substrate Preparation

Paddy straw and growing substrate are cleaned, treated, and packed into bags — the base every mushroom grows from.

2

Spawning

Quality oyster mushroom spawn is layered into the substrate under clean conditions to avoid contamination.

3

Incubation

Bags rest in a dark, temperature-controlled room while the mycelium spreads fully through the substrate.

4

Hi-Tech Fruiting

Bags move into our hi-tech growing rooms where humidity, temperature, and light are closely controlled to trigger healthy fruiting.

5

Harvest

Mushrooms are hand-picked at peak freshness across our 1,500-bag setup, in flushes over several weeks.

6

Pack & Deliver

Freshly harvested mushrooms are packed the same day and sent out — no long cold storage, no chemical preservation.
